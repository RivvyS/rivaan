Who are programmers?

A programmer is a person who writes code 24/7. A person who is ALWAYS in front of his computer, in an effort to create apps, games and sometimes even websites like this one. Basically, a programmer is a person who is extremely smart and brainy, yet doesn't have a life!

Types of programmers

App Developer

I'm sure you spend a good portion of your day using apps such as youtube, netflix, spotify and sometimes even google maps! Well, who do you think made all these??? APP DEVELOPERS!

Game Developers

A game developer is a person who codes interactive games, which, all of us enjoy playing all day long

Web Developers

Web developers are usually never considered as "CODERS" but it isn't very easy. All the websites you visit... somebody made them!
